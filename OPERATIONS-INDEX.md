### For New Team Members
1. Read OPERATIONS-DELIVERY-SUMMARY.md (overview)
2. Review OPERATIONS-QUICK-REFERENCE.md (cheat sheet)
3. Read OPERATIONS-RUNBOOKS.md FAQ section
4. Go through daily checklist

1. Print OPERATIONS-QUICK-REFERENCE.md

5. Respond to alerts <15 min

2. Use weekly metrics template
3. Review incident logs monthly
4. Update runbooks quarterly
5. Certify team members

The Method

How presence is made

Four disciplines from raw silicone to named individual — applied to every body in the range.

01 · Measurement

Every body is a real architecture

Before a character is cast, the body receives a full spec card: bust, waist, hip, height, cup, and two signature ratios — WHR and BWR. Not estimates. Precise measurements that define a silhouette signature no other body shares.

WHR (waist-to-hip) determines the core drama of a figure. BWR (bust-to-waist) locates the upper architecture. Together they place each body uniquely in a space no marketing language can substitute.

27 distinct architectures · each with a named spec card
02 · Body Families

Six measured silhouettes

Architectures are grouped into six families — not by aesthetic whim, but by measurement signature. The Classic. The Icon. The Muse. The Siren. The Empress. The Sculpt.

Each family has a distinct proportion envelope: a WHR range, a cup cluster, a characteristic bust drop. A body belongs to its family because the numbers place it there, not because a copywriter named it.

6 families · defined by measurement, not mood
03 · Characters

Four named individuals per architecture

Each body architecture carries exactly four characters — four head/face pairings, each with a name, a title, a tagline, and a persona direction. You choose a person, not a part number.

No two characters within a body share a face code. No character is nameless. The slot system (№ 1–4) signals positioning within the architecture, from editorial to expressive.

— characters · up to 4 per body · each named and directed
04 · Material

Platinum silicone, handfinished

Platinum-cure silicone — prop-grade, stable, and alive to the touch in a way no TPE blend replicates. The same material used in film effects production, poured into architectures defined by measurement rather than market convention.

SLE construction removes the dead weight. A movable EXP jaw adds the small expression shifts that read as presence. Handfinished skin detail is applied last — because a measured body without craft is still just a form.

Platinum silicone · SLE core · EXP movable jaw

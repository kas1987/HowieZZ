## Executive Summary

The ZELEX Community Hub is a three-pillar platform designed to amplify collector voice while maintaining brand safety. The system launches with:

- **Gallery:** Verified collector photo submissions with 24-hour moderation

### 2. Events Calendar ✅

**Files:**
- `community-events.html` — Events calendar page (107 lines)
  - Calendar subscription UI (Google Calendar, .ics download)
  - 6 recurring events with full details
  - RSVP tracking (current vs max attendees)
  - Event categories (onboarding, editorial, community, meetup, workshop)
  
- `db/community_events_calendar.json` — Calendar data (104 lines)
  - 6 recurring events with detailed metadata
  - Start/end times in UTC + timezone info
  - Recurring rules (MONTHLY, BI-MONTHLY, QUARTERLY)
  - Location, description, RSVP CTA, attendee tracking
  
- `community-calendar.ics` — iCalendar export (110 lines)
  - Generated by build script
  - 6 VEVENT entries with RRULE recurrence
  - Compatible with Google Calendar, Outlook, Apple Calendar, etc.
  - Auto-generated DTSTAMP for cache busting
  
- `scripts/generate_ical.py` — iCal generation (146 lines)
  - Converts `community_events_calendar.json` to RFC 5545 format
  - Escapes special characters for iCal compliance
  - Generates proper DTSTART/DTEND in UTC
  - Handles recurring event rules (FREQ, INTERVAL, COUNT)

**Events (6 recurring):**
1. **Collector Onboarding Office Hours** — 1st Sunday, 14:00 UTC (Monthly)
   - Discord #onboarding | 50 max attendees | 12 current RSVPs
   - New collector orientation, channel etiquette, verification

2. **Magazine Issue Preview** — 2nd Sunday, 16:00 UTC (Monthly)
   - Zoom + virtual meetup | 100 max | 34 RSVPs
   - Upcoming issue preview, submission voting, cover design

3. **Safety + Verification AMA** — 3rd Sunday, 15:00 UTC (Bi-monthly)
   - Discord #ama-general | 200 max | 87 RSVPs
   - Impersonation prevention, handle verification, moderation Q&A

4. **Photography Workshop: Studio Lighting** — 4th Sunday, 13:00 UTC (Monthly)
   - YouTube Live + Discord | 75 max | 41 RSVPs
   - Professional photography techniques, lighting setup, post-processing

5. **Customization Show & Tell** — 1st Saturday (after), 17:00 UTC (Monthly)
   - Discord #showcase-gallery | 150 max | 52 RSVPs
   - Custom mods, materials, techniques, gallery highlights

6. **Collector Meetup: North America** — 2nd Friday, 18:00 UTC (Quarterly)
   - In-person regional meetups | 200 max | 78 RSVPs
   - Meet-and-greet, collection showcase, exclusive merchandise

**Calendar Features:**
- ✅ Google Calendar sync (1-click subscribe)
- ✅ .ics download for manual import
- ✅ Timezone conversion guidance (UTC/EST/PST)
- ✅ RSVP capacity tracking
- ✅ Event category filtering (by mode: Discord, Editorial, etc.)

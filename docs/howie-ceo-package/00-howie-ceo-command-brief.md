## Page 1 - What You Should Decide Now

### Board Summary

ZELEX should run a concentration-first launch, open Classic immediately, and keep Sculpt in pilot containment until proof thresholds are met.

### The Core Call

Run a focused launch, not a full six-family promotion.

- Keep Icon and Muse as the two primary revenue families.
- Keep Siren active in discovery, but not as a homepage hero.
- Keep Empress discoverable but secondary while depth is thin.
- Treat Classic as the top product emergency.
- Keep Sculpt in pilot mode only until differentiation is validated.

This gives ZELEX the strongest balance of immediate conversion and strategic expansion without diluting buyer clarity.

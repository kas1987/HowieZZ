## What This PDR Covers

This record documents every material decision in the ZELEX family activation initiative — from the market intelligence work already completed, through the dead-end elimination actions required now, through the manufacturing intelligence layer that compounds the value of those investments over time. Each decision point presents the available paths with their trade-offs.

This is not a roadmap of things to do. It is a map of choices, with the costs and consequences of each path made visible so the right sequence can be selected deliberately.

## Decision Point 3 — Manufacturing Model for Production

This governs how the factory stages inventory. Three models exist. The choice determines lead time, inventory risk, and competitive positioning.

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    subgraph MTO["Model A — Make-to-Order (Status Quo)"]
        MTO_HOW["How it works:\nOrder arrives → work order generated\n→ Factory starts from skeleton\n→ 8–12 weeks to ship"]
        MTO_PROS["PROS\n✓ Zero inventory risk\n✓ No capital committed pre-order\n✓ Full personalization (all variables open)"]
        MTO_CONS["CONS\n✗ 8–12 week lead time is\n  industry standard — no differentiation\n✗ Factory utilization uneven\n✗ Customer experience: long wait"]
    end

    subgraph BTS["Model B — Build-to-Stage (Proposed)"]
        BTS_HOW["How it works:\nDemand signal (quiz/cart)\n→ Pre-stage L1 skeleton + L2 pour\n→ Order arrives → L3 skin + L4 head only\n→ 2–3 weeks to ship"]
        BTS_PROS["PROS\n✓ 2–3 week lead time vs. 8–12 week industry\n✓ Lead time becomes product differentiator\n✓ Moderate inventory risk (L1+L2 only)\n✓ Personalization preserved at L3+L4\n✓ Data-driven: demand signals drive queue"]
        BTS_CONS["CONS\n✗ Requires demand signal infrastructure\n✗ Some L1+L2 inventory may not convert\n✗ Needs quiz + cart data integration\n  (Phase 1: market estimates as proxy)"]
    end

    subgraph PRE["Model C — Pre-Built Finished Inventory"]
        PRE_HOW["How it works:\nBuild finished dolls to forecast\n→ Ship in 1–2 weeks from stock"]
        PRE_PROS["PROS\n✓ Fastest possible ship time\n✓ Simple operations"]
        PRE_CONS["CONS\n✗ Skin tone + head locked in before order\n✗ High returns risk on mismatched personalization\n✗ Heavy capital in finished goods\n✗ Wrong SKU mix = unsellable stock\n✗ NOT viable for configurable products"]
    end

    MTO --> RECOMMEND_B["Model B is the target state.\nModel A is the starting state.\nModel C is not viable for configurable products."]
    BTS --> RECOMMEND_B
    PRE --> RECOMMEND_B
```

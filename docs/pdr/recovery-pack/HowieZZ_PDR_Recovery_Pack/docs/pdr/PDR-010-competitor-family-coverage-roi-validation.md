# PDR-010: Competitor Family Coverage and ROI Validation

## Objective
Validate whether the six-family taxonomy has real commercial ROI by comparing Zelex coverage against competitor product families.

## Core question
Does the market cluster into the same 2-3 body families, or do competitors cover families Zelex does not?

## Scope
- Collect 80 minimum body profiles; 150+ preferred.
- Include Zelex baseline plus at least 6 competitors.
- Calculate WHR, BWR, family assignment, confidence, and source.
- Produce brand-by-family coverage matrix.
- Score ROI by family.
- Recommend primary, secondary, in-development, or suppressed family status for launch.

## Required competitor set
ZELEX baseline, RealDoll / Realbotix, Irontech, WM Doll, Piper Doll, Doll Forever, Tayu, Sanhui, 6YE / JY or substitute if data availability requires.

## Acceptance criteria
- Every profile has source URL and measurement basis.
- Every profile has family assignment or explicit unclassified reason.
- Report identifies whitespace and launch hierarchy.
- CEO-facing recommendation is included.

## Dependencies
PDR-009 and research templates.

## Owner
Financier + Auditor + Archivist.

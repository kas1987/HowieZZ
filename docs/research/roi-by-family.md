## Scoring Methodology

Each of the six ZELEX body families is scored 1–5 across eight dimensions defined in the PDR-010 design spec. Higher is better.

| Dimension | What it measures | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| Product availability | Current ZELEX body count and readiness | 2× |
| Competitive differentiation | How distinct ZELEX is in this lane vs. the 29-brand market | 1× |
| Buyer demand signal | Evidence of active buyer intent in this silhouette category | 1× |
| SEO and content opportunity | Difficulty-adjusted organic reach potential | 1× |
| Conversion utility | Ability to drive quiz → product page → inquiry flow today | 2× |
| Visual merchandising value | Hero image, editorial, and social potential | 1× |
| Sales qualification value | Utility for routing the right buyer to the right product | 1× |
| Future product roadmap value | Strategic importance for catalog expansion | 1.5× |

**Composite formula:** Sum of (score × weight) ÷ total weight (10.5)

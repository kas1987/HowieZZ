Community Guidelines

  • Photos must feature ZELEX products or collector setups.
  • Include a brief description and relevant tags (body family, location, etc.).
  • No political, adult, or hateful content. Follow regional laws for shared photos.
  • Credit other collectors or artists in your description if inspiration is drawn from their work.
  • Approved submissions get featured in the gallery and may be shared to official social channels.
